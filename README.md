# AI Operations Hub

**Document Automation That Keeps Your Data Private**

AI Operations Hub is a B2B SaaS platform that automates document processing, email triage, and internal knowledge management — all running on your infrastructure with no data sent to external servers.

## Live Demo

**[View Live Site →](https://sreejagatab.github.io/AI-Operations-Hub-demo/)**

## Features

- **Document Processing** — Classify and extract data from invoices, contracts, BOLs, and more
- **Email Automation** — Triage emails, draft responses, extract action items
- **Knowledge Base** — Answer staff questions from your internal policies with source citations
- **100% On-Premise** — Your data never leaves your servers. No OpenAI, no Google, no third parties.
