1. Introduction

AI Operations Hub ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, platform, and services (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Service, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Account Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Company name
  • Job title
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Billing address
  • Payment information (processed by our payment provider)

Communications

  • Emails and messages you send us
  • Support tickets and inquiries
  • Feedback and survey responses

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data: Features accessed, actions taken, date and time of access, session duration, and error logs.

Device Information: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, and device identifiers.

Cookies: Session cookies, preference cookies, and analytics cookies.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

Your Documents and Data

When you use our On-Premise Deployment:

  • We do NOT have access to documents you process
  • We do NOT collect or store your processed data
  • We do NOT transmit your documents to our servers
  • Your data remains entirely on your infrastructure

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who assist us with payment processing, email delivery, analytics, customer support, and infrastructure. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information to comply with applicable laws, respond to valid legal process, protect our rights, or enforce our Terms of Service.

4.3 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you of any such change.

4.4 What We Never Do

  • We NEVER sell your personal information
  • We NEVER share your information for third-party advertising
  • We NEVER use your documents to train AI models
  • We NEVER access your on-premise data without explicit permission

5. Data Retention

Account Information: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 90 days after termination.

Billing Records: Retained for 7 years (legal requirement).

Communications: Retained for 3 years after resolution.

Your Documents: For On-Premise Deployment, your documents remain on your infrastructure. We have no access to them.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+)
  • Encryption at rest for stored data
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Employee security training
  • Incident response procedures
